# PRD: Płyndo.pl - Corporate Identity & Landing Page ## 1. Brand Identity Overview - **Name:** Płyndo.pl - **Industry:** Household chemicals sold in packages for homes and smaller businesses - **Heritage:** Polish cleaning expertise presented through the Płyndo.pl package offer - **Vibe:** Minimalist, Digital, Clean, Trustworthy, AI-Driven. ## 2. Visual Identity (Zadanie 1 & 2) ### Color Palette - **Primary (Digital Clean):** #007AFF (Electric Blue - symbolizing water, tech, and the ".pl" domain) - **Accent/CTA (Energy):** #FFFD00 (Neon Yellow/Lime - high visibility, fresh) - **Background (Technical White):** #F9FAFB (Off-white, clean, technical) - **Text (Soft Dark):** #1A1C1E (Deep Charcoal - friendly yet authoritative) ### Typography (Google Fonts) - **Headlines:** **Plus Jakarta Sans** (Modern, geometric, excellent for display) - **Body:** **Inter** (Industry standard for readability and UI performance) ### Logo Concept - **Logotype:** Lowercase "płyndo" in a thick, rounded sans-serif. The ".pl" is integrated into the "o" or as a superscript that mimics a digital notification badge. - **Symbol (Sygnet):** A stylized drop of liquid with a subtle "+" or sparkle inside (AI/Professional strength). ## 3. Landing Page Structure (Zadanie 3) 1. **Hero Section:** - **H1:** Płyny do domu i firmy w pakietach. - **Sub:** Poznaj Starter 10 albo dobierz paczkę 4 lub 8 płynów do realnego zużycia. - **CTA:** Wybierz swój pakiet. 2. **Heritage/Trust:** Jakość, polski kontekst i wygoda dostawy ciężkich płynów. 3. **AI Assistant ("Doradca Czystości"):** Interactive wizard (Home size -> Pets -> Recommended Package). 4. **Product Grid:** 8 key products with minimalist labels and color coding. 5. **Package Guide:** 3-step visual guide (Choose -> Review contents -> Continue to Shoper). 6. **FAQ & Footer.** ## 4.
